about the staff

Hi My Name is Jacquelyn! I’m an artist with too much time on my hands.
The Armpit Gallery is an old garage I have reclaimed into an artspace. A lot of the work you see is not just curation and gallery admin but also renovation. It’s been an absolute journey to curate this show with not much more than $75 and a shawarma. I couldn’t have done this without my Gallery Assistant Lucy (pictured above) and all of my good friends.
