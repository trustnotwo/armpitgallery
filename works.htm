seething : malding


appropriating Richie Culver’s iconic “born died” (2023) work; seething : malding centers on the variety of coping mechanisms we endure. From illicit substances to questionable morality, anger and melodrama perform as undercurrents in the lives of 11 emerging artists based in the midwest.
